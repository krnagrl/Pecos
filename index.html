Pecos

Overview

Pecos is a diversified business group involved in multiple industries such as securities, hospitality, drinks, and other sectors. Established in 2020 by Karan Agrawal, the company has grown significantly across these sectors.

Business Operations

  • Securities: Pecos offers a range of financial products and services, including investment advisory and portfolio management.
  • Hospitality: The company owns and operates a number of luxury hotels and resorts across the country.
  • Drinks: Pecos is involved in the production, distribution, and marketing of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.
  • Other Sectors: The company is exploring opportunities in new industries, including technology and real estate.

Controversies

Due to its involvement in several heavily regulated industries, Pecos has faced scrutiny. The company emphasizes compliance with regulations and adheres to ethical business practices to mitigate concerns.
