Pecos

Industry: Securities, Hospitality, Drinks

Founded: 2020

Headquarters: Margao, Goa, India

Website: www.pecos.com

Overview

Pecos is a small, emerging business group based in Margao, Goa, with operations in the securities, hospitality, and drinks industries. Founded in 2020, the company is in its early stages but has shown great promise in building a niche market.

Business Operations

  • Securities: Pecos offers limited but growing financial products, primarily focused on investment advisory and savings plans for individuals.
  • Hospitality: The company runs a small boutique hotel in Goa, offering a personalized experience to tourists looking for a quieter, more intimate stay.
  • Drinks: Pecos is in the early stages of developing a unique local beverage line, focusing on health-conscious drinks and organic ingredients.

Interesting Facts

  • Startup Culture: As a small company, Pecos thrives on a close-knit team and fosters a startup culture with a focus on agility and innovation.
  • Local Focus: Pecos places a strong emphasis on local sourcing, working closely with local farmers and suppliers to create sustainable products.
  • Personalized Hospitality: The boutique hotel owned by Pecos is known for its highly personalized services, aiming to provide a more intimate experience than large hotel chains.
  • Community Support: Pecos engages in small-scale community initiatives, including local clean-up drives and supporting local schools through donations.
