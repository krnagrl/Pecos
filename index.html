Pecos

Industries: Real Estate, Mining, Entertainment, Logistics, Finance, Food & Drink, Retail, Education, Healthcare, Engineering

Founded: 2020

Headquarters: Margao, Goa, India

Overview

Pecos is a dynamic and diversified business group based in Margao, Goa. Since its founding in 2020, it has expanded rapidly across ten major industries, emphasizing local growth, sustainability, and innovation.

Business Operations

  • Real Estate & Construction: Development of residential and commercial properties with a focus on green building practices.
  • Mining: Involved in ethical mineral extraction and resource management across select regions in India.
  • Entertainment: Produces regional films and digital content, supporting local talent and stories.
  • Logistics: Offers efficient logistics and supply chain services across Goa and neighboring states.
  • Finance: Provides financial services, including microloans and investment consulting for small businesses and individuals.
  • Food & Drink: Develops local food products and beverages with a focus on organic and health-conscious ingredients.
  • Retail: Operates a chain of retail outlets featuring local products and sustainable goods.
  • Education: Runs training centers and schools focused on vocational and digital skills development.
  • Healthcare: Manages clinics and mobile healthcare units targeting rural and underserved areas.
  • Engineering: Offers civil, mechanical, and renewable energy engineering services to regional clients.

Interesting Facts

  • Diversified Portfolio: Pecos is one of the few small business groups in India with a footprint across 10 diverse industries.
  • Local Empowerment: The company employs a high percentage of local talent and partners with local suppliers and service providers.
  • Green Initiatives: Emphasizes eco-friendly practices across real estate, engineering, and food operations.
  • Community Engagement: Regularly supports education, health camps, and environmental campaigns across Goa.
