Pecos

Industry: Securities, Hospitality, Drinks, and more

Founded: 2020

Headquarters: Margao, Goa, India

Website: www.pecos.com

Overview

Pecos is a diversified business group involved in multiple industries such as securities, hospitality, drinks, and other sectors. Established in 2020, the company has grown significantly across these sectors.

Business Operations

  • Securities: Pecos offers a range of financial products and services, including investment advisory and portfolio management.
  • Hospitality: The company owns and operates a number of luxury hotels and resorts across the country.
  • Drinks: Pecos is involved in the production, distribution, and marketing of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.
  • Other Sectors: The company is exploring opportunities in new industries, including technology and real estate.

Interesting Facts

  • Global Expansion: Despite being a relatively young company, Pecos has already begun exploring international markets for its hospitality and drinks sectors.
  • Eco-Friendly Initiatives: Pecos is committed to sustainability and has launched several initiatives aimed at reducing its carbon footprint, such as using renewable energy in its hospitality facilities.
  • Innovation in Drinks: Pecos is known for its innovative approach to the beverage industry, experimenting with new flavors and unique ingredients that set its products apart in the market.
  • Community Engagement: Pecos believes in giving back to the community and actively supports various social causes, including education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.
