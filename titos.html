About Us

Tito's is a local-style Goan restaurant and bar inspired by traditional coastal hospitality and relaxed tropical living.

Located beside a peaceful roadside surrounded by coconut trees, our place serves fresh seafood, local Goan curries, cocktails, chilled beer and authentic coastal flavours.

Whether you're stopping during a beach drive or enjoying a late-night dinner with friends, Tito's brings you the natural vibe of Goa.
