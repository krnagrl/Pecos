Pecos

Type: Private Company

Industry: Tobacco, Gambling, Finance, Alcohol

Founded: 2020

Headquarters: Akola, Maharashtra, India

Key People: Karan Agrawal (Founder)

Website: www.pecos.com

Overview

Pecos is a diversified business group involved in multiple industries such as tobacco, gambling, finance, and alcohol. Established in 2020 by Karan Agrawal, the company has grown significantly across these sectors.

Business Operations

  • Tobacco: Pecos produces and distributes a wide range of tobacco products.
  • Gambling: The company has investments in casinos and online gaming platforms.
  • Finance: Pecos offers financial services such as lending, investment advisory, and asset management.
  • Alcohol: Pecos is involved in the production and distribution of premium alcoholic beverages.

Controversies

Due to its involvement in sectors like gambling and alcohol, Pecos has faced regulatory scrutiny. The company emphasizes compliance and responsible business practices to mitigate such concerns.
