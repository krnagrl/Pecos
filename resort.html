Sunkissed Resort is a charming Goan-style tropical retreat designed for travellers seeking relaxation, nature and authentic coastal experiences.

Surrounded by swaying coconut trees and peaceful roadside scenery, our resort combines modern comfort with traditional Goan architecture.

Enjoy calm mornings, sunset evenings and a warm atmosphere perfect for couples, families and vacation travellers.
