Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Pecos?
    Pecos is a professional services company providing solutions in finance, P2P lending, investment management, broking, and real estate insights.
  • Do I need an account to access Pecos services?
    No, we currently provide informational access without requiring account creation. For partnership or service inquiries, you may contact us directly.
  • Can I lend money through Pecos?
    Pecos does not directly facilitate financial transactions. We provide educational information and connect you with regulated platforms where required.
  • Are your services regulated?
    Where applicable (e.g., financial advisory or lending facilitation), we work only with registered entities under Indian regulatory authorities such as SEBI or RBI.
